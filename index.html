Software is as essential to research as telescopes, test tubes, and reference libraries. However, most researchers are never shown how to develop and deliver software efficiently as part of a team. This one-day workshop teaches participants how to structure a project, automate repetitive tasks, ensure that what they build is doing what it's supposed to, coordinate contributors, and build the right thing.

Syllabus

  • Introduction
  • Governance
  • Workflow
  • Automation
  • Quality
  • Structure
  • Sharing
  • Leadership
  • Conclusion
